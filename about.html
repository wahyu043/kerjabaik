About KerjaBaik

Learn more about our story, our people, and our vision to empower organizations and individuals to thrive.

Our Story

KerjaBaik is a training & consulting company dedicated to helping individuals and organizations grow in today’s workplace. From the very beginning, we believed that everyone has untapped potential that can be unlocked with the right approach.

Founded by Cahya Maulana, an expert with over 7 years of experience in corporate training and consulting, KerjaBaik has supported state-owned enterprises, government institutions, and private companies in building stronger, more resilient human capital.

We are committed to continuously improving our services by adopting innovative methods, strengthening our team, and leveraging digital technology. For us, empowering organizations means enabling the next generation to face workplace challenges with confidence.

Meet Our Founder

Cahya Maulana

With more than 7 years of experience delivering impactful training and consulting programs, Cahya has worked with state-owned enterprises, government institutions, and private companies across Indonesia. His vision for KerjaBaik is to create a people-centered, practical, and scalable learning partner for organizations.

Under his leadership, KerjaBaik continues to innovate and expand, building solutions that empower both individuals and companies to thrive in an ever-changing world of work.
